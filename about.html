About Saranya

Saranya’s quest and desire into dancing took her path towards Trichy to make a first milestone as she enrolled into a five year course (2005-2010) of integrated bachelor of fine arts degree in Bharatanatyam with Kalai kaveri College of fine arts, affiliated to Bharathidasan University and she completed the degree with a first class and Gold medal.  Saranya continued her passion further by completing Masters Degree in Bharatanatyam in Kalai Kaveri College of fine arts and completed it with Distinction.  Over the years, Saranya’s Guru in  Bharatanatyam is Srimathi.Ogeswari Shanmuganathan, a disciple of kalashethra   and former professor in Kalai Kaviri College of fine arts. Saranya has also completed a certified degree in folk dance in Kalari Panpaatu Maiyam, Nagercoil.
Saranya holds a diploma degree in yoga.
Saranya completed a three years part time course in Mohiniyattam, a classical dance of Kerala under her Guru  Srimathi.Kumari Menon a student of Kalyani Kuttiyamma Kerala Kalamandalam.
Saranya learned Kuchipudi from her Guru Natya Kala Arasan Sri. Thavarajah Mohanapriyan. She completed her one year diploma in Kuchipudi in first class at Kalai Kaviri College of fine arts.
Saranya has completed a diploma in Dance Teacher Training with first class in The Tamil University, Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu.  br Being an eminent member of Kalai Kaveri College of fine arts, Saranya was one of the main dancers of Kalai Kaveri dance troupe from 2006 to 2012 and has performed more than 500 stage performances across India, Germany, Netherlands.
Saranaya has her career in Teaching Bharathanattiyam as well and worked as a part time lecturer from 2007 to 2012 for students doing Diploma in Bharathanattiyam in Kalai Kaveri College of fine arts. As a motivating teacher Saranya trained 28 students in their Diploma in Bharathanattiyam and successfully guided them to perform their Arangetrams. Saranya has also done Creative dance therapy on education in Kalai Kaveri College of fine arts.
Saranya is currently working as a Dance teacher in DISHA -A life school in Pollachi, Tamil Nadu and continuing to train young budding students in Bharatanatiyam.
Akhila Bharatha Ayyappa Seva Sangam has greatly appreciated Saranya on her performance during the Bharatanatyam Festival in 2011 and has described as her performance has enlightened the gathering towards culture, spiritual tradition and togetherness which are greatly appreciated by the distinguished audience at Bilaspur, Chattisgarh State.
Saranya has huge devotion and passion in Bharatanatyam and wishes to bring refreshing treat and a dance to remember for her esteemed audiences.
