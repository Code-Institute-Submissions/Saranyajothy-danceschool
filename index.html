Anugraha Bharatanatyam School

Anugraha Bharatanatyam school trains children and adults with basic and advance classes in Bharatanatyam, which is a traditional and popular South Indian dance. The vision of Anugraha Bharatanatyam School is to pass on the heritage to next generation while keeping the age old tradition to preserve the culture. We are committed to take the classical dance to new generations by welcoming modernisation, fushion and creativity. Where appropriate, we blend the dance with modern technology to bring the divine art to global audience and younger generation. Anugraha is one of the finest dance schools in Ireland and dedicatedly providing classes in Cork and Limerick. Over the past eight years the students of Anugraha have performed in various events and stages.

Students of Anugraha Bharatanatyam School have access to various learning methodology like theory behind the dance, its history, practical classes, dance workshops and educational trips to various dance concerts. At Anugraha Bharathanatyam School we encourage continuous and global learning by collaborating with various dance schools in Ireland and across the globe.

Students of Anugraha Dance school have received appreciation and awards from University College of Cork, World Malayalee Council and various Indian Diaspora Community groups.

Classes

The classes are being conducted in Cork and Limerick. Classes are more focused on syllabus with progression from one lesson to the next. Each class will ensure perfection of momements and lessons such as Adavu (steps), Hastas (hand gestures), yoga, thala, jathis, theory and dance songs.
